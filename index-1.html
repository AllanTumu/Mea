About Us

Mea is a ugandan based brand that deals in Fashion for now, with hopes of venturing into events, Entertainment and Media in the future. This company was started by a ugandan entreprenuer whose dream is to create as many jobs a possible and extend services and basic needs such as health care, education and many others to the less priviledged people mostly those in remote areas of the country.

Allan Tumuhimbise who is the C.E.O of Mea group of companies and the founder of MeaWear comes from a humble background,he decided to venture into business so that he could earn a living. This has taught him a lot of business dynamics thus starting these companies to create employment and also do charity through these ventures
